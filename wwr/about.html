History

In the spring of 2018, NS Rafting embarked on its exhilarating journey along the roaring rivers, tracing its roots back to a passionate group of outdoor enthusiasts who shared a deep love for adventure and nature. The company was born out of a vision to provide thrilling and safe rafting experiences, showcasing the stunning landscapes and untamed waters that define the heart of the outdoor adventure scene. On a memorable day, the founders officially launched NS Rafting, marking a significant date in the calendar of adrenaline seekers.

Over the course of the past years, NS Rafting has rapidly evolved into a premier destination for white-water enthusiasts, earning a reputation for its commitment to safety, experienced guides, and unforgettable journeys down some of the most challenging rapids. The company has expanded its fleet of state-of-the-art rafts, diversified its range of trips to cater to various skill levels, and established a strong community of thrill-seekers who return time and again. With a focus on sustainable tourism and a passion for preserving the natural beauty that serves as its backdrop, NS Rafting continues to ride the waves of success, offering unforgettable adventures and forging lasting memories for all who dare to join the ride.
